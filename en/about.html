Company Profile

From the showroom in Amreya to the factory in Borg El Arab

Oscar Poultry Supplies has been active since 1980, building long-term experience in poultry equipment, supplies, and practical products that serve daily farm operation as well as dealer and distributor needs.

The showroom is located in Amreya, Alexandria, while the factory is based in Borg El Arab. This gives the business a stronger operational presence across display, preparation, supply, and wider order fulfillment for both local demand and Arab export markets.
